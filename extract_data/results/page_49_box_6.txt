NOTE: 4" and 6" Pulls use 8-32 x 1”screws and mount on .75”thick doors/cabinets. 
  
12" and 18" Pulls use 10-24 x 1.3”screws and mount on doors between .75" to 1" thick. 
 
Pulls come with optional base plates to sit beneath the pull legs. 
 
This is not required and is included for an optional design aesthetic.
